World's youngest Microsoft Certified Professional,2004–2008

Abdul Karim Randhawa (Punjabi: عرفہ کریم رندھاوا‎; 2 February 1995 – 14 January 2012) was a Pakistani student and computer prodigy who became the youngest Microsoft Certified Professional (MCP) in 2004. She was submitted to the Guinness Book of World Records for her achievement.
